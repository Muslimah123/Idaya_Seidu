My Journey into Data Science and Product Management

Hello everyone! Welcome to my personal blog. I'm Idaya Seidu, an aspiring data scientist and product manager. Today, I want to share with you my journey into the fascinating world of data science and product management, and what has inspired me to pursue a career in these fields.

My Background

My journey began with a strong interest in technology and a desire to solve real-world problems. I completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Science at Ashesi University, where I developed a solid foundation in software development and database management. During my time at Ashesi, I participated in various projects and internships that allowed me to apply my skills and gain practical experience.

One of the most impactful experiences was my internship as a research assistant at AMMREN, where I worked on malaria data access. This project not only honed my technical skills but also sparked my interest in using data to drive decisions and create meaningful impact.

Discovering Data Science

My curiosity about data science grew as I learned more about the field and its potential to transform industries. I enrolled in several online courses to deepen my knowledge, including "Machine Learning with Python" and "Python for Data Science" offered by IBM. These courses provided me with a comprehensive understanding of machine learning algorithms, data visualization techniques, and the tools necessary to analyze and interpret complex datasets.

Through these studies, I realized that data science was not just about crunching numbers; it was about uncovering insights and telling a story through data. This realization motivated me to pursue a career in data science, where I could leverage my analytical skills to solve challenging problems.

Transitioning to Product Management

While data science was my primary focus, I also became intrigued by the role of product management. I was fascinated by the idea of being at the intersection of technology, business, and user experience. As a product manager, I would have the opportunity to shape the development of products from inception to launch, ensuring they meet user needs and drive business success.

To gain experience in product management, I worked on several projects that required me to understand user requirements, define product features, and collaborate with cross-functional teams. These experiences allowed me to develop essential skills in project management, stakeholder communication, and strategic thinking.
